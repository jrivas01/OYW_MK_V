Technical and Historical Notes
The MS-06J Zaku II is a minor adaptation of the MS-06F, intended for land combat. The J-type Zaku II is almost exactly identical to the F-type; the visible exceptions being purely superficial, including three spikes on its shield and more pronounced wrists.

The J-type Zaku II eliminates much of the space-exclusive hardware of the F-type, including its enhanced self-sealing components around the cockpit, atmospheric filtration, etc. Also, the F-type Zaku's calf thrusters have been replaced with larger, more directional thrusters in its feet.

The J-type features an improved gyroscope for land combat, and is somewhat more maneuverable in a terrestrial environment than the F-type. The extra weight saved by phasing out unnecessary gear gives the Zaku slightly more armor than its universal counterpart.

The land combat Zaku arms the same weaponry as the F-type, but is much more commonly equipped with cracker grenades than other models. It can also optionally arm a pair of 3-tube missile launchers strapped to its legs. These launchers would significantly slow a space use model, but are a minor hindrance in a terrestrial environment.


Weapons and Features
ZMP-50D 120mm Machinegun - 120mm machinegun. Note the optical scope and ammunition drum.

H&L-SB25K 280mm Zaku Bazooka - 280mm "Zaku" bazooka. Same optical scope as the 120mm, with a rear-loading magazine.

Type 5 Heat Hawk - Heat hawk. A superheated hatchet for melee combat.

Leg Missile Launcher - 3-tube missile launcher used for long distance fire support, can fire straight or in an angle much like a conventional howitzer.

Grenades - Standard use Zaku grenade as well as the more infamous "Cracker" grenade.

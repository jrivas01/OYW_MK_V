Technical and Historical Notes

A conventional cargo airliner, this aircraft, modeled after the old Russian AN-224, is capable of operating much like a Medea, without any armament and a reduced hangar capacity. Unfortunately, unlike the Medea it is unable to parachute its hangar compliment, having to land in order to disembark its mobile suits.

Technical and Historical Notes

A small bulk transport craft used by both the Colony Management Corporation and the Earth Federation, the aptly dubbed "Cargo Transport Craft" has roughly five times the cargo capacity of your average shuttle, while maintaining similar maneuverability and speed. Like with most civilian vessels, it is unarmed.

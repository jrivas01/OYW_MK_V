Technical and Historical Notes
The MS-06F2 is the second generation version of the Zaku II F, Zeonics new design an overall improvement of the old model.

The Zaku II F2 carries very traditional Zaku armaments, including the Heat Hawk and cracker grenades, and options to store missile launchers or sturm fausts on the legs. In addition, the Zaku II F2 was rolled out with improved anti-mobile suit weaponry, including the Zaku Kais MMP-80 machinegun and a new model 120mm machinegun with the rare ability to fire multiple types of shell.

The F2 is an improvement over the traditional F type in many ways, not the least of which is its weaponry. It is considerably lighter, mounts an improved reactor, and much more powerful directional thrusters, making it more maneuverable in space and giving it a much better ability to make boost-assisted jumps on land. Its armor is equivalent to the Zaku II Fs, although the armor around the cockpit has been increased. Further, the F2's cockpit has been redesigned, the newer model giving much better overall control of the mobile weapon.

Although it was released late in the war, the Zaku II F2 was a considerable improvement over the Zaku II F, and considerably easier for green pilots to use, making it a much needed asset for late-war Zeon.


Weapons and Features
MMP78 machinegun - 120mm machinegun. Can fire either 120mm drum shells or 90mm MMP-80 shells.

MMP80 machinegun - 90mm machinegun. Arms a single grenade in a detachable launcher.

H&L-SB25K 280mm Zaku Bazooka - 280mm "Zaku" bazooka. Same optical scope as the 120mm, with a rear-loading magazine.

Type 5 Heat Hawk - Heat hawk. A superheated hatchet for melee combat.

Grenades - Standard use Zaku grenade as well as the more infamous "Cracker" grenade.

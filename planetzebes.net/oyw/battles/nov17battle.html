Battles - November 17th

AN AMBUSH! How Dreadful.


Jane was hunting and her targets were close. A sandstorm had spontaneously swirled up in the late afternoon, blanketing the land with a sea of fine particulate matter. Above the dust cloud it was bright and clear. A lone Luggon patroled, watching for enemies and seeing none. Jane used the provided cover well. Fortunately, her thermal sensors worked perfectly in the dust storm. She was dug into a small pit just ahead of the Zaku's predicted line of travel.

"450meters, closing." she said quietly.

Jane toggled a switch on her right side, triggering micro corrections for windage and lead. She had her bazooka leveled on the edge of the pit waiting. The two Zaku's emerged from the relative cover they had been afforded by the sand. Their mission was complete and Private First Class Till was leading leading his troop back to have maintenance done.

"God forsaken sand, I wish we could go somewhere green." Ernest grumbled. "This place is a nightmare..."

Jane waited, patiently, breathing slowly to calm her nerves. At once a a shadow crossed Janes viewfinder, "There you are." Jane noted at the Luggon high above. The pilot crossed the sun and silhouetted himself to her, a rookie mistake. "Perfect, all the pieces are in place." she said smiling a little. The situation was textbook, the pair of enemies were oblivious and she had them dead to rights. Steeling her nerves, Jane took the shot. The first round from her bazooka ripped out of the prepared position, and screamed into its target. An up armored Zaku J, painted half green and half tan- pitched forward, its lower right leg completely blown away.

"CONTACT!" Ernest yelled over his throat mic at his Luggon overhead.

"Sir, we have nothing on the scopes!" the Luggon pilot yelled back.

"This is garbage, I can't see anything!" Back-pedaling, Ernest leveled his machine gun into the dust ahead and poured out suppressing fire. Jane threw aside the bazooka and leapt from the earthen pit at the enemy Zaku. Tracers ripped by her flying GM, some scoring hits on the armor but doing no real damage damage. Jane landed hard, skidding across the sand only 20 meters from the Zaku. She unslung her machine gun and leveled the weapon on her hip. She opened fire, pouring rounds into the direction of Ernies Zaku. Within seconds she had expended her magazine, dropped her first and slammed another into the weapon. Ernie fell back into the haze, his mobile suit peppered with holes, virtually disabled and unable to return effective fire. Jane, satisfied with the carnage fell back to her position, mission accomplished.

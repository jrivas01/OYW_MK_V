Battles - February 3rd

Back Room Brawl

It was dark in one of the storage rooms aboard the Ol' Jove's Bones. The door slides open as the light from the hallway shone in and two shillouetes entered. The door closes shut and the lights power on, blinding one of the men who entered. Putting an arm up to shield his eyes from the light until he adjusted to them, Jacobin Conroy looked around the room they entered. A multitude of crates filled the room, though packed together so tightly that it made more room than would be expected. Turning back to the man who led him inside here, he spoke up, "You mean we're to... Here?"

Hands behind his back, the second man retorted, "My lad... Ye really question why I bring ye here?" The Wraith, Captain Samuel Riley, eyes closed and raising his brow, questioned his subordinate. "Now, listen 'ere. We're 'ere to hone those skills ye call yer own." Riley grinned and released his hands from behind his back and gripped the rapier at his side. Pointing it at Jacobin, he motioned towards his opponent to draw his own weapon. "Show me what a bilge rat can do."

Taking his own rapier from his side, Jacobin got into stance, immediately feeling the reason why his captain chose this room. Fighting in zero gravity. A confined area, but enough room to move about. Nodding to his opponent, showing he was ready, Jacobin lunged forward with his rapier. However, putting too much energy into his body's motion, he lost balance and started to fall forward.

Taking a lithe step to the side, Riley chuckled. "Lad, you know better to get used to your battlefield before engaging in combat! Now, come at me again!" He said as he took a swipe of his rapier through the stale air of the room and readying it again. Jacobin got back up to his feet after a nearby crate cushioned his 'fall' and moved towards Riley with a thrust, this time most of the force behind just the thrust and no heavy follow-throughs. A light connection of metal was heard as Riley swiped his rapier against Jacobin's, parrying his attack. Immediately recovering from the parry, Jacobin pulled back his arm and then swung out his rapier again overhead of Riley, only to be met with another parry.

Jacobin noticed that Riley was a little slow this time. No, he was getting used to his environment. With a grin, Jacobin started to lash out at Samuel Riley, causing his opponent to retreat as they both left the ground of the storage room. The clash of metal got louder and faster as they both started exchanging blows, not letting up on one another. Jacobin took one last rear of his rapier and swung it at Riley with a bit too much force. Riley fended off the attack only to be pushed back into the upper corner of the storage room, putting quite a gap between him and Jacobin.

Letting out a hearty laugh, the Captain shook his head. "Looks I 'ave this match, boy." The comment bought Riley some time as he pulled in his legs, turning and pushing himself off the upper corner of the room, rapier ready to be thrust. Seeing his opponent ready to 'jump' at him, Jacobin realized it was all too late to even do anything to react as he saw The Wraith soar right towards him, the rapier leaving a cut across Jacobin's left arm as Riley passed. "Ye lost, lad." Samuel Riley claimed victory in their spar as he looked up at his fellow pirate. If that had been a real battle, y'd be dead!" Smiling, he opened the door to the storage room and let is slide close behind him, leaving Jacobin alone to reflect.

Outcome: Samuel Riley Victory
Samuel Riley: 5 CP Gained
Jacobin Conroy: 3 CP Gained, Injured 1



Reach out and touch someone!

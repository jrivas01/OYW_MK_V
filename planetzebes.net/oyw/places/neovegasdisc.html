Neo Vegas & Neo Hong Kong
Side 6 Bunch 4
Mirrored Colony Cluster

The site of both Side 6's largest black market and industrial complex, as well as the massive trading center where the Jupiter Energy Fleets organizes the transfer of its all important cargo, Neo Vegas and Neo Hong Kong have made a name for themselves as one of Side 6's more important colony clusters. Heavily populated, the night life on Neo Vegas is much more prominant than on the majority of colonies.

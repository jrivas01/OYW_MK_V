Bases

The compound links below are listed by their relative location, and annotated with country flags to make identifying them easy on the eyes. Where a location is controlled by one side, flag annotations will also be put around the location header. PC owned bases are listed in italics.

Each compound entry links to a separate page with detailed information on the compound, and each header links to MS team patrols in that area. Individual entries alternate between present defenses and descriptions/map via links on the names.

Positions on this page that do not have flag annotations are Territories, and cannot be controlled.
